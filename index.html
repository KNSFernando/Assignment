Scribble

Role: Software Engineer

The project is about a game of Scribble. It is quite like the game of Scrabble. The purpose of the game of Scribble is for players to take turns making words from a collection of alphabetical letters that are available to them. On their turn, a player can place a sequence of letters onto the spaces of a game board to form a single word. Each letter of the alphabet has points associated with it that will get added to the player’s score. Once a player has placed their word onto the board, the points for each letter they have used are calculated and then added to the player’s total score. The winner of the game will be the player who has the most points at the end. I really doubt it will happen since I have set a limit of 200 turns for the game to end. Therefore, it is highly likely that the game will end when a player gets 100 points.


Key Challenge: I faced quite a few challenges during the coding process. Major one being the shear size of the code I was writing. At one point, it was over 5000 lines of code! It was quite daunting to just go through my code. This happened because I wrote every player’s turn down in my method. Since it was the player's turn that caused the mountain of code, I solved it by making the player’s turn a method of its own.
